SDG 7 Targets

SDG 7 aims to ensure access to affordable, reliable, sustainable, and modern energy for all. The targets below outline specific goals to achieve by 2030 and track our global progress towards a sustainable energy future.

Target 7.1: Universal Access

By 2030, ensure universal access to affordable, reliable, and modern energy services.

Global Progress: 89%

Key Focus: Electrification in rural areas and clean cooking solutions for all.

Learn More

Target 7.2: Renewable Energy

By 2030, increase substantially the share of renewable energy in the global energy mix.

Global Progress: 17%

Key Focus: Solar, wind, hydroelectric, geothermal, and other renewable energy technologies.

Learn More

Target 7.3: Energy Efficiency

By 2030, double the global rate of improvement in energy efficiency.

Global Progress: 32%

Key Focus: Improving energy intensity across buildings, industry, transportation, and appliances.

Learn More

Target 7.A: International Cooperation

By 2030, enhance international cooperation to facilitate access to clean energy research and technology.

Annual Investment: $18.6B

Key Focus: Technology transfer, research funding, and knowledge sharing across borders.

Learn More

Target 7.B: Infrastructure Expansion

By 2030, expand infrastructure and upgrade technology for supplying modern and sustainable energy services.

Developing Countries: 53%

Key Focus: Energy infrastructure in least developed countries, small island states, and landlocked countries.

Learn More

SDG 7 Implementation Timeline

2023-2024

Policy Development Phase

Countries develop national clean energy policies and infrastructure investment frameworks aligned with SDG 7 targets.

2025-2026

Implementation & Integration

Implementation of renewable energy integration into existing grids and expansion of access in underserved areas.

2027-2028

Scaling & Acceleration

Scale up energy efficiency programs and transition to clean cooking solutions globally.

2029-2030

Target Achievement

Final push to achieve universal access to affordable, reliable, sustainable, and modern energy for all.
