Overview

Affordable and clean energy is energy that costs less, harms the planet less, and can be used by many people. It helps fight climate change, saves money, and improves lives. Here is a simple list of examples:

  • Solar power (using sunlight)
  • Wind power (using wind)
  • Hydropower (using water)
  • Geothermal energy (using Earth's heat)
  • Energy efficiency (using less energy)

Affordable and clean energy is a cornerstone of global efforts like the United Nations' Sustainable Development Goal 7 (SDG 7), aiming to ensure access to affordable, reliable, sustainable, and modern energy for all by 2030.
